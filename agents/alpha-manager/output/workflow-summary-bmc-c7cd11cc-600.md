## 1. Niches Selected & Rationale

Based on available technical feasibility research (Workflow 6fa6a62c-86a, Beta Manager), selected two complementary high-scoring opportunities:

- **Score:** 8.2/10 (Exceptional)

- **Target:** B2B mid-market financial advisors
- **Appeal:** High ACV ($500-2K/mo), strong moats (compliance), growing regulatory pressure
- **Validation Cost:** $36K estimated to test

### Niche 2: Creator Operations Micro-SaaS
- **Score:** 7.5/10 (Strong)
- **Model:** Unified operational tools for content creators
- **Target:** B2C solopreneur creators ($1K-10K/mo revenue)
- **Appeal:** Viral growth potential, lower CAC, creator economy tailwinds
- **Validation Cost:** $24.5K estimated to test

**Selection Criteria:**
- Complementary risk profiles (B2B stable vs B2C fast-growth)
- Different validation paths (sales-led vs product-led)

- Diverse market timing (regulatory tailwind vs creator economy boom)
