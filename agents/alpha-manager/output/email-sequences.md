# Email Marketing Sequence Templates

---

## Part 1: Nurture Sequence (5 Emails)

### Email 1: Lead Magnet Delivery
**Send:** Immediately after opt-in
**Goal:** Deliver promised value + build trust

**Subject:** Your [Lead Magnet Name] is ready 📥
**Preview Text:** Here's the resource you requested + a quick tip to get started...

---

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for requesting [Lead Magnet Name]. I've attached it here.

**Quick start tip:** Don't try to implement everything at once. Start with Section 3 — that's where most people see results within the first week.

Hit reply if you have questions. I read every email.

Best,  
[Your Name]

**P.S.** I'll send you a few follow-up emails with case studies and practical tips. If they're not helpful, you can unsubscribe anytime.

**Attachment:** [Lead Magnet Name]

---

### Email 2: Education
**Send:** 2 days after Email 1
**Goal:** Provide value + establish expertise

**Subject:** The mistake most [target audience] make with [topic]
**Preview Text:** It seems obvious once you see it — but most people miss this entirely...

---

Hi [First Name],

I was reviewing some client files last week and noticed a pattern.

Most [target audience] approach [topic] backward. They focus on [common mistake] when they should be prioritizing [better approach].

Here's the difference:

**❌ The wrong way:** [Explanation of common mistake and why it fails]

**✅ The right way:** [Explanation of better approach and why it works]

I wrote about this in detail [link to blog post / case study]. It's a 5-minute read that could save you hours.

Questions? Just reply.

[Your Name]

---

### Email 3: Social Proof
**Send:** 4 days after Email 2
**Goal:** Build credibility through results

**Subject:** How [Client Name] [achieved result] in [timeframe]
**Preview Text:** No tricks or shortcuts — just a systematic approach that actually works...

---

Hi [First Name],

Wanted to share a quick win from [Client Name], a [brief description of who they are].

**The challenge:**
[Describe their situation in 2-3 sentences — be specific about the pain point]

**The approach:**
[Describe the solution in 2-3 sentences — focus on process, not just outcomes]

**The result:**
[Specific, quantifiable outcome + timeframe]

Here's what [Client Name] had to say:

> "[Brief testimonial quote — authentic, not polished to perfection]"

Full case study here: [link]

Talk soon,  
[Your Name]

---

### Email 4: The Offer
**Send:** 3 days after Email 3
**Goal:** Present solution without being pushy

**Subject:** A practical way to [solve their problem]
**Preview Text:** This isn't for everyone — but if you're dealing with [specific pain point], it's worth a look...

---

Hi [First Name],

By now you've seen the [lead magnet], read about the [common mistake], and heard how [Client Name] turned things around.

If you're struggling with [specific pain point], I want to make sure you know about [Your Service/Product].

**What it is:**
[2-3 sentence description focusing on outcome, not features]

**Who it's for:**
- [Target audience characteristic 1]
- [Target audience characteristic 2]
- [Target audience characteristic 3]

**Who it's NOT for:**
- [Someone who isn't a fit 1]
- [Someone who isn't a fit 2]

**How it works:**
[Brief 3-step process or explanation]

If this sounds relevant, here's where to learn more: [link to sales page / booking page]

No pressure either way. Just want to make sure you know it's an option.

[Your Name]

---

### Email 5: Urgency / Last Call
**Send:** 2 days after Email 4
**Goal:** Create gentle urgency + final opportunity

**Subject:** Last call: [deadline / reason to act]
**Preview Text:** [Price/deal/availability] changes tomorrow — or if that's not relevant, here's something else...

---

Hi [First Name],

Quick heads up: [specific reason for urgency — deadline, price change, limited spots, etc.]

**If [Your Service/Product] is relevant to you:**
[Clear call to action with link]

**If the timing isn't right:**
No worries at all. I won't keep emailing you about this.

**If this isn't a fit, period:**
Hit unsubscribe below. No hard feelings — I'd rather have a smaller list of people who find this useful.

Either way, thanks for reading.

[Your Name]

## Part 2: Onboarding Sequence (3 Emails)

### Email 1: Welcome
**Send:** Immediately after purchase/signup
**Goal:** Confirm decision + set expectations

**Subject:** Welcome to [Product/Service Name] — let's get you set up
**Preview Text:** Your account is ready. Here are your next steps...

---

Hi [First Name],

Welcome to [Product/Service Name]. I'm glad you're here.

**What happens next:**

**Step 1:** [First action — e.g., "Log in here" with link]

**Step 2:** [Second action — e.g., "Complete your profile" or "Schedule your kickoff call"]

**Step 3:** [Third action — e.g., "Join the community" or "Download the app"]

**Your login details:**
- Username: [email/username]
- Portal: [link to login]
- Password: [instructions to set/reset]

**Need help?**
- Knowledge base: [link]
- Email support: [email]
- Response time: [X hours/days]

Looking forward to working with you.

[Your Name]
[Your title/role]

---

### Email 2: Setup Guide
**Send:** 1 day after Email 1
**Goal:** Reduce friction + drive first success

**Subject:** Your [Product/Service] setup checklist — start here
**Preview Text:** Don't skip Step 2. Most people do, and it makes everything harder...

---

Hi [First Name],

Yesterday you got access to [Product/Service Name]. Today, let's make sure you're set up for success.

**Quick-start checklist:**

**☐ Step 1:** [Setup task — e.g., Connect your calendar / Import your contacts / Install the extension]

**☐ Step 2:** [Critical setup task — this is the one people skip!]

**☐ Step 3:** [Quick win task — something they can complete in 5 minutes]

**☐ Step 4:** [Optional but recommended task]

[CTA Button: "Complete Setup Now →"]

**Time estimate:** 10-15 minutes

**Not sure where to start?**

[Link to 2-minute setup video]

Most people who skip this step end up [consequence of not doing setup]. Take 10 minutes now — you'll thank yourself later.

Questions? Just reply.

[Your Name]

---

### Email 3: Tips & Resources
**Send:** 3 days after Email 2
**Goal:** Drive engagement + prevent churn

**Subject:** 3 tips for getting the most from [Product/Service]
**Preview Text:** Most people don't know about #2 — it saves about 2 hours per week...

---

Hi [First Name],

You've been using [Product/Service Name] for a few days. Here are 3 quick tips to help you get more value:

**Tip 1: [Feature/Workflow Name]**
[One sentence on what it does] + [one sentence on how to access it]

**Tip 2: [Keyboard Shortcut / Automation / Integration]**
[Explanation of how this saves time] + [how to set it up]

**Tip 3: [Advanced Feature or Best Practice]**
[Why this matters] + [link to tutorial or knowledge base article]

**Need help?**

- 📚 Knowledge base: [link]
- 📹 Video tutorials: [link]
- 💬 Support: [email] or [phone]

**One question:**

What's your biggest goal with [Product/Service] right now? Just hit reply and let me know — I read every email and often send helpful resources based on what people tell me.

Thanks,  
[Your Name]
