## 3. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

### 3.1 ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES
**NEVER use our Services for:**
- **Violating any law:** Local, state, national, or international laws
- **Fraud:** Schemes, deception, or fraudulent activities
- **Unauthorized access:** Hacking, credential stuffing, or system intrusion
- **Illegal commerce:** Buying/selling illegal goods or services
- **Money laundering:** Concealing origins of illegally obtained money
- **Terrorism/violence:** Planning, supporting, or glorifying violence

### 3.2 CHILD SAFETY
**ZERO TOLERANCE for:**
- CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material)
- Grooming or sexual exploitation of minors
- Content sexualizing children
- Trafficking of minors
- Any content that endangers children

**Violation = Immediate termination + Law enforcement referral**

### 3.3 HARMFUL OR DANGEROUS CONTENT
**Prohibited:**
- Content promoting self-harm or suicide
- Instructions for creating weapons or harmful devices
- Content encouraging eating disorders
- Instructions for illegal acts
- Content that could cause physical harm if followed

### 3.4 HATE SPEECH AND HARASSMENT
**Prohibited:**
- Content attacking protected groups (race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability)
- Organized hate speech or hate groups
- Doxxing or publishing private information
- Coordinated harassment campaigns
- Stalking or threatening individuals
- Non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII)

### 3.5 MISINFORMATION AND MANIPULATION
**Prohibited:**
- Deceptive AI-generated content (deepfakes) without clear disclosure
- Election disinformation or voter suppression
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Impersonation of real individuals without consent
- Medical misinformation that could cause harm
- Financial scams or market manipulation

### 3.6 INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY INFRINGEMENT
**Prohibited:**
- Copyright infringement (pirated content, unauthorized distribution)
- Trademark infringement or counterfeiting
- Circumventing DRM or technical protections
- Distributing content you don't have rights to
- Training on copyrighted works without authorization

### 3.7 PRIVACY VIOLATIONS
**Prohibited:**
- Processing EU residents' data without GDPR compliance
- Collecting biometric data without consent
- Using facial recognition on non-consenting individuals
- Scraping personal data from users
- Credential harvesting or phishing

### 3.8 PLATFORM ABUSE
**Prohibited:**
- Exceeding rate limits or quotas
- Circumventing security measures
- Reverse engineering or decompiling
- Tampering with monitoring or logging
- Creating fake accounts or botnets
- Manipulating leaderboards or metrics
- Automated abuse of free tiers

### 3.9 RESOURCE ABUSE
**Prohibited:**
- Crypto mining without authorization
- Excessive automated requests (DDoS-like behavior)
- Intentionally consuming disproportionate resources
- Bypassing usage limits
- Sharing accounts to circumvent quotas

### 3.10 MALICIOUS CODE
**Prohibited:**
- Creating or distributing malware
- Developing ransomware tools
- Creating botnets or command infrastructure
- Keyloggers or spyware
- Exploit development or distribution

## 4. AI-SPECIFIC RESTRICTIONS

### 4.1 Automated Decision-Making
When using AI for consequential decisions:
- Provide human review opportunities where required by law
- Disclose automated decision-making to affected parties
- Document decision rationale for appeals
- Maintain audit logs

### 4.2 High-Risk Applications
**Enhanced restrictions apply to:**
- Credit/lending decisions
- Employment screening
- Housing decisions
- Healthcare diagnoses
- Legal/judicial applications
- Public safety/security

### 4.3 Prohibited AI Uses
**Cannot use Platform AI for:**
- Surveillance without legal basis
- Social scoring systems
- Deceptive practices (undisclosed bots)
- Generating realistic false evidence
- Creating fake official documents
- Automated decision-making on protected characteristics

## 6. ENFORCEMENT

### 6.1 Escalation Tiers

#### Tier 1: Warning
**Applied for:**
- First-time minor violations
- Unintentional policy breaches
- Over-quota usage (1st offense)

**Actions:**
- Email warning
- Usage restrictions may apply
- No account action

#### Tier 2: Temporary Suspension
**Applied for:**
- Repeated Tier 1 violations
- Serious policy violations
- Automated abuse patterns

**Actions:**
- 7-30 day suspension
- Access blocked during suspension
- Requires acknowledgment of policy

#### Tier 3: Permanent Termination
**Applied for:**
- Egregious violations
- Repeated serious violations
- Illegal activity
- CSAM
- Harassment campaigns

**Actions:**
- Permanent account closure
- Forfeiture of prepaid credits
- Data retention per legal requirements only
- Law enforcement referral if warranted

### 6.2 Appeal Process
Users may appeal enforcement actions within 30 days:
1. Submit appeal via support@[platform].com
2. Include explanation and context
3. Appeals reviewed within 14 business days
4. Decision communicated via email

**Note:** Decisions on CSAM, illegal activity, and imminent harm are final.
