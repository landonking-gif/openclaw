## Detailed Building Profiles

### 1. Burj Khalifa (Dubai, UAE)
- **Height:** 828 m (2,717 ft)
- **Construction Period:** January 2004 - October 2009
- **Duration:** ~5.75 years
- **Cost:** $1.5 billion (AED 5.5 billion)
- **Notable:** World's tallest building since 2010; 163 floors
- **Source:** CTBUH Skyscraper Center, Wikipedia

### 2. Merdeka 118 (Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia)
- **Height:** 679 m (2,227 ft)
- **Construction Period:** July 2014 - November 2023
- **Duration:** ~9.3 years
- **Cost:** RM5 billion (~$1.1 billion USD)
- **Notable:** Second tallest building in the world; 118 floors
- **Source:** Wikipedia, Skyscraper Center

### 3. Shanghai Tower (Shanghai, China)
- **Height:** 632 m (2,073 ft)
- **Construction Period:** November 2008 - August 2014
- **Duration:** ~5.8 years
- **Cost:** $2.4 billion (CN¥15.9 billion)
- **Notable:** China's tallest building; 128 floors; LEED Platinum
- **Source:** Wikipedia, Asharq AlAwsat

### 4. Abraj Al Bait / Makkah Royal Clock Tower (Mecca, Saudi Arabia)
- **Height:** 601 m (1,972 ft)
- **Construction Period:** 2002 - 2011
- **Duration:** ~9 years
- **Cost:** $15 billion (SAR 56 billion)
- **Notable:** World's most expensive building; 120 floors; largest clock face
- **Source:** Wikipedia, cnVision (mover.uz)

### 5. Ping An Finance Center (Shenzhen, China)
- **Height:** 599 m (1,966 ft)
- **Construction Period:** January 2010 - March 2017
- **Duration:** ~7.25 years
- **Cost:** $1.5 billion
- **Notable:** Fourth tallest building; 115 floors
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 6. Lotte World Tower (Seoul, South Korea)
- **Height:** 555 m (1,821 ft)
- **Construction Period:** April 2011 - December 2016
- **Duration:** ~5.7 years
- **Cost:** $2.9 billion
- **Notable:** Tallest building in South Korea; 123 floors
- **Source:** Wikipedia, Skyscraper Center

### 7. One World Trade Center (New York, USA)
- **Height:** 541 m (1,776 ft - symbolic of US independence year)
- **Construction Period:** April 27, 2006 - July 2013
- **Duration:** ~7.3 years
- **Cost:** $3.9 billion
- **Notable:** Tallest building in Western Hemisphere; 104 floors
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 8. Guangzhou CTF Finance Center (Guangzhou, China)
- **Height:** 530 m (1,739 ft)
- **Construction Period:** 2009 - October 2016
- **Duration:** ~7 years
- **Cost:** $1.5+ billion (est.)
- **Notable:** 111 floors; mixed-use tower
- **Source:** Wikipedia, Skyscrapers.cc, Skyscraper Center

### 9. Tianjin CTF Finance Centre (Tianjin, China)
- **Height:** 530 m (1,740 ft)
- **Construction Period:** January 3, 2013 - September 22, 2019
- **Duration:** ~6.7 years
- **Cost:** ~$1.5 billion (est.)
- **Notable:** 97 floors; distinctive curved glass facade
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 10. CITIC Tower / China Zun (Beijing, China)
- **Height:** 528 m (1,732 ft)
- **Construction Period:** May 19, 2012 - September 30, 2018
- **Duration:** ~6.4 years
- **Cost:** ~$1.5-2 billion (est.)
- **Notable:** Tallest in Beijing; 109 floors; rooftop helipad
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 11. Taipei 101 (Taipei, Taiwan)
- **Height:** 508 m (1,667 ft)
- **Construction Period:** July 31, 1999 - December 31, 2004
- **Duration:** ~5.3 years
- **Cost:** $1.9 billion (NT$58 billion)
- **Notable:** World's tallest 2004-2009; 101 floors; tuned mass damper
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 12. Shanghai World Financial Center (Shanghai, China)
- **Height:** 492 m (1,614 ft)
- **Construction Period:** August 27, 1997 - 2008
- **Duration:** ~11 years (delayed 1997-2003 due to Asian Financial Crisis)
- **Cost:** $1.20 billion (CN¥8.17 billion)
- **Notable:** 101 floors; distinctive bottle opener shape
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 13. International Commerce Centre (Hong Kong, China)
- **Height:** 484 m (1,588 ft)
- **Construction Period:** 2002 - 2010
- **Duration:** ~8 years
- **Cost:** ~$1+ billion (est.)
- **Notable:** 108 floors; Ritz-Carlton occupies top floors; 28 floors skipped due to tetraphobia
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 14. Wuhan Greenland Center (Wuhan, China)
- **Height:** 476 m (1,562 ft) - reduced from original 636 m due to airspace regulations
- **Construction Period:** June 28, 2012 - 2022
- **Duration:** ~10 years (multiple delays)
- **Cost:** $4.5 billion
- **Notable:** Redesigned mid-construction; COVID-19 pandemic delays; 101 floors
- **Source:** Wikipedia

### 15. Central Park Tower (New York, USA)
- **Height:** 472 m (1,550 ft)
- **Construction Period:** September 2014 - 2020
- **Duration:** ~6 years
- **Cost:** $3 billion
- **Notable:** Tallest residential building in the world; 98 floors
- **Source:** Wikipedia, NBC New York

### 16. Lakhta Center (St. Petersburg, Russia)
- **Height:** 462 m (1,516 ft)
- **Construction Period:** October 30, 2012 - 2019
- **Duration:** ~7 years
- **Cost:** $1.77 billion
